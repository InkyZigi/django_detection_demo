About Us

Why we build such a intelligent system for defect detection?

Surface anomaly detection based on computer vision has gradually become one of the significant directions for the intelligent application in industrial production. Traditional solutions for defect detection are mostly built upon image processing technology or machine learning algorithms, but rely heavily on large amounts of supervised data. The deep learning anomaly detection algorithm dominated by generative adversarial networks and autoencoders has good performance. The knowledge distillation detection technology based on pre-training networks fully utilizes the prior knowledge of upstream models, achieving better performance.

This project combines techniques like with techniques such as pre-training networks, knowledge distillation and feature pyramid, and adopts a self-supervised anomaly detection method based on Student-Teacher Feature Pyramid Matching. The method has achieved excellent pixel-level performance on industrial image datasets. Based on the research of algorithms, this article attempts to combine image processing technology with deep learning detection technology, adopts front-end, back-end and database technology, focuses on detection efficiency and data management, and designs an intuitive, feature-rich, easy-to-use and easy-to-maintain intelligent industrial vision system for defect detection. Users can obtain data management services and image detection results through a concise view, ultimately realizing the enhancement of industrial vision and the intelligence of defect detection.
